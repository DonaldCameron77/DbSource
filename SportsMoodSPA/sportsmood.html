The Adventure Begins!!!!!!!!

Welcome! Perhaps you're like many sports fans who over the years have come to care about certain baseball teams, and to despise those teams that beat their favorites.

Now you can see, for any given day on the MLB schedule, how you should feel based on how those teams you love - and hate - did that day.

Click Help to get started, or Settings if you are already among the cognoscenti of the diamond!


Please note:

  • An active Internet connection is required.
  • Game results are limited to two days, August 29th and 30th, 2015.
  • Session Storage is used to retain settings. It will disappear when your tab is closed.

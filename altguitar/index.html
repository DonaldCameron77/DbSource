About This Site

As a student at (among other places) Free Code Camp, when I learned a 'tribute site' challenge had been added to the curriculum, it was a golden opportunity to delve further into the ($mouthful_alert) modern acoustic fingerstyle genre. I had encountered the music of Kaki King and Andy McKee before, and I was aware that the roots of the modern style might lie in the old Windham Hill Records slate of guitarists, but this genre is far enough from the mainstream that I had forgotten who to search for when I wanted to check it out further. Fortunately, some friends at The Gear Page were able to help me, and once I had rediscovered King and the long-forgotten Michael Hedges, finding more than enough additional artists and great tracks was easy.

Technical note: this site is a simple one-page front-end-only affair with no JavaScript. Bootstrap 3 is the foundation for styling, layout, and mobile-friendliness.
